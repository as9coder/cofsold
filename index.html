WHO IS SOLDIER BOY?

Soldier Boy (real name Ben) is the original superhero — the very first Compound V-enhanced individual created by Vought-American. As the leader of Payback (the precursor to The Seven), he was America's golden boy in the 1940s-80s. Beneath the star-spangled bravado lies a ruthless, traumatized killer who will do anything to reclaim his legacy.

📜

HISTORY

Created by Vought in 1944, Soldier Boy was the prototype for all modern supes. He led Payback through decades of missions — both real and fabricated. After a disastrous mission in Nicaragua (1984), he was betrayed by his own team and captured by the Russians, where he was held and experimented on for 40 years before being freed in the present day.

🎭

PERSONALITY

Arrogant, charismatic, and dangerously unstable. Soldier Boy is a walking contradiction — a patriotic icon who despises modern America, a hero who revels in collateral damage, and a leader who demands absolute loyalty while giving none. His time in Russian captivity has left him with severe PTSD and an uncontrollable rage.
